2. TECHNICAL DESCRIPTION

2.1 Particular & design criteria

2.1.1 Refer to the table in Section 6.

2.1.2 The electric motor for each pump shall have a minimum reserve power of 10% for the entire

discharge range of the pump.

2.1.3 Electric motor shall be supplied by the Pump Supplier, however, the Motor Supplier shall be

designated by the Buyer.

The electric motor for the pump shall have sufficient power for throughout the operating range of

pump.

The reserved power shall be informed at quotation.

2.1.4 Main LO pump shall be designed to have the rated capacity at 100cSt and shall not be overloaded at

260 cSt.

2.1.5 The speed of centrifugal pumps shall NOT exceed 1,800 rpm except for the following pump of

which speed is maximum 3,600 rpm :

- FW hydrophore pump

- Ejector pump for FW generator

-Jacket cooling FW preheating pump for main generator engine

- Feed water pump for auxiliary boiler

- Boiler water circulation pump

- Jockey pump

- Hot water circulating pump

- Clean drain pump

- Cooling FW booster pump for package air cond. unit

2.1.6 Pump shall be designed to have the “NPSH required” of 3.5 m, unless otherwise specified.

However, the following pump shall have the “NPSH required” of 2.0 m :

- Feed water pump for auxiliary boiler

- Boiler water circulation pump

2.1.7 The pumps shall be suitably selected to have the operating point near maximum efficiency point on

performance curve (Q-H).

The cavitation should not happen within ±10 m head tolerance from the design point on performance

curve (Q-H).

2.2 Vibration

The Supplier shall be responsible for any kind of harmful or excessive vibration of the pump, exceeding

the allowable vibration limit required by the Buyer as follows :

- 7.5 mm/s, peak for internally excited vibration

- 11.5 mm/s, peak for externally excited vibration

Precaution shall be taken to achieve low vibration levels.

The requirement on the vibration mentioned on the General Specifications shall be applied to the relevant

components.

2.3.12 The pressure gauges shall be liquid filled type.

2.3.13 Sensing line for FO and LO pressure sensors and gauges shall be of seamless steel or stainless steel

tubes.

2.3.14 Working range of pressure gauge shall be marked with green color. Exact range shall be informed at

drawing approval stage by the Buyer.

2.3.15 Pump shall have constantly rising head versus capacity characteristics from rated capacity to zero

capacity and non-overloading power characteristics.

2.3.16 The self-priming type pump shall be provided with the self-priming unit(vacuum pump type) which has the adequate capacity for system requirement.

2.3.17 The protection cover for rotating parts of vertical centrifugal pumps shall be provided according to

SOLAS requirements (SOLAS II-1, part C. Req. 26 Item 1).

Protection guard for coupling shall be provided at the all position.

2.3.18 Piping

Materials and dimensions of pipes, joints, valves and fittings shall be according to the KS, JIS,

ANSI, DIN or BS.

All external pipe connections of nominal diameter 15A(1/2 inch) and above shall be of flange type.

Where flanges not manufactured to the recognized national standards are fitted, counter flanges

(slip-on type) with bolts/nuts and gaskets shall be supplied by the Supplier.

Class certificate or test report for counter flanges as required shall be submitted to the the Buyer.

For tubing connections of nominal diameter below 15A (1/2 inch), appropriate counter connectors

of bite type shall be fitted by the Supplier.

The tubes of ND 15 mm and below shall be of seamless copper in general.

The fittings, joint connectors and valves shall be of screw or bite connection of brass or stainless

steel.

The gauge line of ND 15 mm and below in FO, LO, hydraulic oil and starting air system shall be of

stainless steel.

The fittings, joint connectors and valves shall be of screw or bite connection of stainless steel.

List of all connections including typical drawing, kind, size, pressure and type shall be submitted in

the approval drawing.

2.3.19 Power consumption shall be constantly decreased when flowrate is reduced to zero capacity.
