2. TECHNICAL PARTICULARS

2.1 Electric power source.

- Main : 440V, 60Hz, 3 Phase.

- Control : 220V, 60Hz, 1 Phase.

2.2 The fan casing shall be treated with shop primer and inside and outside of fan casing shall be

painted as follows ;

- Casing inside : Tar epoxy coating.

- Casing outside : Supplier should apply paint according to buyer’s latest version of painting

specification.

follows ;

- Fan casing : Steel

- Impeller : Aluminum alloy

- Wire mesh at air inlet : SUS 316

- Shaft : S45C

- Others : Supplier’s standard

2.4 Particulars and Design criteria

2.4.1 All electric parts shall be satisfied with electric general specifications.

2.4.2 The design and manufacture of the products shall be in accordance with good marine practice to

ensure reliability and easy maintenance.

2.4.3 All fans shall be provided the terminal box and board (water tight type) with cable gland of

constructed cast iron or steel plate.

2.4.4 Vent. fan casing shall have suitable maintenance hatch(water tight) for motor inspection.

2.4.5 Lifting eyes shall be fitted to heavier fitting for the sake of installation work and transportation above 30 kg.

2.5 Material and Construction

2.5.1 The material of explosion proof type fan. shall be non-sparking type and shall be satisfied with

concerned rule and regulation mentioned general term.

2.5.2 Material for the product shall be complied with said rules and regulation except otherwise specified.

2.5.3 Electric fan motor of explosion-proof type shall be installed in outside ventilation ducts.

2.5.4 Wire mesh protection screen of not more than #16-2 mesh(SUS316) shall be fitted as requested by

the Buyer.


2.5.5 Winding type space heater shall be provided for motors exposed to weather deck and required fan

Element type space heater shall be applied to high voltage motors.

2.5.6 The open & close indications to be marked on mushroom vent head by welding bead.

2.6 Requirement of electric design.

2.6.3 Every supplier’s instrument interfaced with yard cable shall have name plate contains their specific name so that every instrument can be distinguishable from each other at on-board.
