If the equipment certified by UR E27, supplier shall provide the document for recovery.

2) A Description of alarms and indicators signaling detected ongoing cyber threat or abnormal behavior

caused by the cyber threat. (threat name, violated firewall rule name, etc.)

3) A Description of expected significant consequences related to cyber incidents (Integrity violation,

Availability violation, Confidentiality violation, etc.)

4) Response options, prioritizing those that do not rely on either shut down or transfer to independent

or local control, if any.

5) Independent and local control information for operating independently from the system that failed

due to the cyber incident, as applicable

2.1.2.6 Recovery plan :

If the equipment certified by UR E27, supplier shall provide the document for recover.

1) Instructions and procedures for restoring the failed system without disrupting the operation

from the redundant, independent or local operation.

2) Processes and procedures for the backup and secure storage of information.

3) Current configuration information for all components.
