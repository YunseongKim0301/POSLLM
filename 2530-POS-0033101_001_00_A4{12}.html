2. TECHNICAL SPECIFICATION

2.1 Particulars and Design Criteria

The Cargo compressor room and electric motor room crane shall be designed for handling of heavy equipment in cargo compressor room and electric motor room.

The crane shall be controlled from the control stand with protection cover on slewing

platform and necessary control and safety devices shall be provided in accordance with

the manufacturer’s standards.

One(1) hydraulic power unit of high pressure type shall be fitted in each crane body.

The crane shall be designed so as to achieve a smooth and high efficient operation.

The crane to consist of jib, pedestal, luffing cylinder, hoisting winch with wire and hook, slewing gear,

hydraulic power pack, access ladder & operation platform, starter & control panel, control system, etc.

Height and direction of the crane jib shall be arranged to minimize obstruction of view from the

wheelhouse. (Yard’s design scope)

Hydraulic piping for the cranes shall be provided according to the manufacturer’s standard.

And the flexible hose connections shall be provided for only at moving sections.

2.3 Material and Construction

2.3.1Crane jib shall be of steel plate construction.

2.3.2The upper part of hydraulic luffing cylinder and the flexible hose connections near the hydraulic

cylinder shall be accessible for maintenance by means of step, ladder or platform, etc.

2.3.3Two(2) eye plates shall be arranged on the jib top for the lifting of jib.

2.3.4The grease pipe shall be extended to the grease nipple located at inaccessible points such as ;

- Upper hinge of luffing cylinder

- Wire sheaves at jib tip (pipe or access platform)

- Grease nipple type & material : ball type (JIS B 1575 PT 1/8”, Material : SUS 316)

2.3.5The size of maintenance hatch shall be suitable for the removal of elec. motor.

The maintenance hatch is at least weathertight and bolted with hinged type.

-Oil resistant rubber(NBR) packing.

-Stainless steel(SUS316) bolts, brass cap nuts and stainless steel(SUS316) washers.

2.3.6 Handrail shall be arranged at both sides to the winch and at one side on other part of jib top for

access.

2.3.7Access platform shall be provided on the crane for maintenance of hoisting winch, which shall be

shown on the drawing.

2.3.8Access shall be considered for all greasing points as well as other parts, where necessary, from the

view point of maintenance, etc.

If unavoidably inaccessible, other substitutional means such as grease piping, etc., shall be applied to

those points/parts.

2.3.9 The position of lifting lug for crane lifting except crane pedestal shall be shown on the drawing.

2.3.10 Exact location and pedestal height of crane shall be informed by the shipyard later on.

2.3.11 The jib cylinders shall be designed for marine use as per the Supplier’s standard.

2.3.12 The material of hyd. pipe except hyd. Fitting shall be of stainless steel (SUS304).

2.3.13 The platform on the crane shall be galvanized.

2.4 Hoisting Equipment

2.4.1 Hoisting winch

The winch unit to consist of the following :

- Drum of grooved type with bearing and brackets (six(6) turn to remain on winch drum)

- Winch gear with hydraulic operated fail safe brake

- Hydraulic motor with safety valve

2.4.2Wire rope

The wire rope shall be of non-rotating type and galvanized steel.

The length of rope shall be sufficient for reaching the bottom of the dry dock when Vessel lies dry

on 2 m blocks.

The minimum safety factor for wire rope shall be 5.

2.4.3Others

The wire sheaves shall be provided with heavy-duty roller bearings on stainless steel axles.

All bearing to have grease nipple for lubrication.

Hoisting winch drum, gear box, hydraulic motor, etc. shall be supplied by the Supplier as complete

unit(fully assembled).

The safety hook shall be provided with latch to prevent the load from slipping off.

Crane hook traveling and hoisting height shall be decided based on the following condition ;

- Location of crane : abt. 36 m above ship’s base line (at the bottom of pedestal)

- Pedestal height : abt. 1.2m (will be informed later on.)

- Six(6) turns to remain on winch drum.

- Crane hook travelling height(hoisting height) : abt.60m (will be informed later on.)

2.5 Control System and Safety Device

2.5.1Control system

The crane with control system shall be so designed that one(1) motion among hoisting, luffing and

slewing can be made possible under the full capacity and full speed, whilst two(2) motions can be

made possible simultaneously under the reduced capacity and the reduced speed in accordance

with the Supplier’s standard.

All motions to have stepless speed control from zero to max.

The crane shall be controlled on the open platform above slewing part, which shall give a good

visibility and accessibility.

The ladder for entrance to the platform with keep chain shall be provided.

The crane shall be capable of inching control.

2.5.2Safety device

The crane to have limit switch, safety device, fail safe function for safety operation shall be provided

in accordance with the requirements of rule and regulations and/or the Supplier’s standard.

The following fittings/devices shall be provided :

- Relief valve for overload and over hoisting

- Emergency stop switch, etc.

- Fail safe function for em’cy situation of power failure or control system failure.

2.6 Hydraulic System

2.6.1Hydraulic power pack

The hydraulic power pack of high pressure shall be installed in crane body.

No leakage line from crane shall be provided.

Electric motor shall be located in the center of the pedestal with shaft and to drive the hydraulic

pump through a flexible coupling.

The slewing column steel structure shall be utilized as tank for the hydraulic oil.

Hydraulic piping for the cranes shall be as per the Supplier’s standard in compliance with the rules.
